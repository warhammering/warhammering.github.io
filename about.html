My tribute to Warhammer

I love programming and making cool webpages, but my passion is Warhammer. I love the hobby very much and have done it for over 10 years now. I am as nerd as I can get when it comes to Warhammer. I have not idea why it pulls me back all the time. So I was more than happy to make a webpage to pay tribute to it

Reasons I love Warhammer

Miniatures

I own over 300 miniatures that I love to put together and paint

The Lore

There is a never-ending of lore and history about the Warhammer universe

Art

So many skillful and creative paintings and digital art exist for it

Roleplaying Games

I can create more stories with tabletop games and funky dices

Console Games

In the last couple of years a huge explosion of games have arrive

Music

As a bi-effect there is now lots of music to listen inspired by the game
